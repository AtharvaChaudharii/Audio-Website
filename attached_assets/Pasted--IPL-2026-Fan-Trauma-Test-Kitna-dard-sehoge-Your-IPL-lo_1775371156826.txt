"IPL 2026 Fan Trauma Test"
"Kitna dard sehoge? Your IPL loyalty is being judged."
🎯 Core Concept
A brutally funny, scroll-based "psychological trauma assessment" for IPL fans — powered by real IPL 2026 events happening THIS WEEK. CSK just lost to PBKS, MI got beaten by DC, Sanju Samson is flopping at CSK, Hardik Pandya benched. The site KNOWS your team. It diagnoses your fan mental health. It roasts you specifically. It's a meme factory you can screenshot every 3 seconds.
🧠 User Experience Flow
Scene 0 — The Disclaimer
Hospital/clinical UI. Siren noise. Red pulsing screen. Warning text types out:
"⚠️ CAUTION: This diagnostic has caused CSK fans to lose sleep since March 22. PBKS fans are annoyingly fine. Proceed at your own risk."
Cursor becomes a cricket bat.
Scene 1 — Team Selection
10 IPL team cards fan out like playing cards. Each card has the team logo + a savage one-liner:

CSK: "Thala for a reason. The reason is suffering."
MI: "5 trophies, 0 wins this week."
RCB: "Loading... loading... (this has been loading since 2008)"
PBKS: "TOP OF THE TABLE?? PBKS?? It's real."
SRH: "226 runs and still anxious. Respect."

User clicks their team. Card explodes into confetti of that team's colors.
Scene 2 — The Diagnosis Scroll
A fake medical "scan" of the user's brain. MRI machine sound effect. Brain highlights regions:

"Toss anxiety region: OVERACTIVE"
"Last-over faith: CLINICALLY ABSENT (CSK fans)"
"Cooper Connolly respect: DEVELOPING"
"Sanju Samson at CSK feelings: COMPLICATED"

Each brain region clickable — popup with meme-format jokes about this week's actual matches.
Scene 3 — The Trauma Timeline
Horizontal scroll like a cricket scorecard. Timeline of THIS WEEK's IPL 2026 pain:

April 1: "LSG 141 all out. KL Rahul golden duck. Goenka sahab's face 😭"
April 2: "SRH 226. KKR fans: 'easy chase' [it was not easy]"
April 3: "CSK vs PBKS. Priyansh Arya 39 off 11. Ma Chidambaram Stadium went quiet."
April 4: "Hardik Pandya injured. Maheika didn't come to watch. Twitter combusted."

Each date card: click to reveal a reaction meme format image (CSS-generated, no copyright).
Scene 4 — The Fan Personality Type
Based on team chosen → personality type reveals with dramatic animation:

CSK fan: "The Eternal Optimist Who Hasn't Slept Since Tuesday"
MI fan: "Trying to manifest the 6th trophy with sheer aggression"
RCB fan: "A poet. A dreamer. Professionally heartbroken."
PBKS fan: "Table topper energy. Still in shock. Refreshing the points table every 5 mins."

Scene 5 — The Prescription
Doctor's prescription pad (skeuomorphic design). Animated handwriting:
Rx for: [User's Team] Fan
─────────────────────────
1. Stop checking Cricbuzz at 2 AM
2. Accept Sanju Samson needs time
3. One (1) cold Thums Up after each loss
4. Strictly NO Twitter after powerplay
5. Repeat: "It's just cricket." [DO NOT BELIEVE THIS]

Dr. Dhoni
MBBS, MS (Chennai Super Kings), PhD (Finishing Games)
Share button generates this as a downloadable prescription PNG.
Scene 6 — The Points Table of Life
A modified IPL 2026 points table — but the columns are:
TEAM | ACTUAL WINS | FAN SUFFERING | MEME OUTPUT | TWITTER FIGHTS | HOPE LEFT
Auto-populated with funny fake numbers. PBKS: Hope Left = 99%. RCB: Hope Left = "Sir yeh toh eternal hai."
